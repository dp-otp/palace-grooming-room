Who We Are

Our Story

Where Precision Meets Prestige

The Beginning

Born in
Wood Green

Palace Grooming Room was born from a passion for precision grooming in the heart of Wood Green, just moments from the grandeur of Alexandra Palace. We set out to create more than a barbershop — a grooming sanctuary where every detail is crafted with intention.

Nestled on Crescent Road, our space blends timeless craftsmanship with modern luxury. From the moment you step through our doors, you enter a world where attention to detail is not just valued — it is everything.
